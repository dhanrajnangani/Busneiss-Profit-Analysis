Profit & Investment Efficiency Analysis
Overview
This project analyzes the relationship between business investments (R&D, Marketing, Administration) and profit across three US states — California, New York, and Florida.
Tools Used

Excel — Regression Analysis (Multiple Linear Regression)
Power BI — Interactive Dashboard & Scatter Plot Visualizations

Key Metrics

Total Profit: $5.60M
Average Profit: $112.01K
Total Investment: $20.30M
ROI: 27.58%

Regression Results

R² = 0.95 — Model explains 95% of profit variation
R&D Spend is the strongest predictor of profit
Marketing spend shows strong positive correlation
Administration spend has minimal impact on profit

Key Insights

New York leads in total profit contribution
Florida delivers the highest incremental ROI
Marketing spend has the strongest association with profit across all states
California shows the lowest profit contribution
